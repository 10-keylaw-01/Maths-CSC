About Our Department

The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science is dedicated to providing high-quality education in the fields of mathematics, computer science, and information technology. Our programs are designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in today's technology-driven world.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty members, and industry partnerships, we ensure our students receive a comprehensive education that prepares them for successful careers or advanced studies. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest developments and industry requirements.

The department maintains strong connections with leading technology companies and research institutions, providing students with internship opportunities and exposure to real-world applications of their academic knowledge.

Rules and Regulations

To maintain academic integrity and ensure a conducive learning environment, all students must adhere to the following rules:

  1. Attendance is mandatory for all lectures and laboratory sessions. A minimum of 75% attendance is required to be eligible for examinations.
  2. All assignments, projects, and laboratory reports must be submitted by the specified deadlines. Late submissions will result in grade penalties.
  3. Academic dishonesty, including plagiarism, cheating in examinations, and falsification of data, is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.
  4. Students must maintain respectful conduct toward faculty members, staff, and peers at all times.
  5. Department resources, including computer laboratories and library materials, must be used responsibly and in accordance with specified guidelines.
  6. Students are required to regularly check their official email and the department notice board for important announcements.
  7. Mobile phones must be silenced during lectures and laboratory sessions.
  8. Food and beverages are not permitted in computer laboratories and lecture halls.
